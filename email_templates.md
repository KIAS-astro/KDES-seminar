# KDES Seminar — Email Draft Templates

Reusable templates for inviting and coordinating with speakers. Adjust names,
dates, topics, and room numbers per occasion.

---

## 1. Invitation

Subject: `Invitation to Speak at KIAS Astrophysics Group Meeting`

> Dear {{Speaker First Name}},
>
> My name is {{Your Name}}, and I am a researcher at KIAS.
> I am writing to invite you to give a talk at one of our upcoming KDESci group meetings.
>
> We would be delighted if you could present your work on {{topic}}.
> ({{optional personal note, e.g. referral or recent achievement}})
>
> Our meetings are informal and usually start at 2:30 pm, with talks running approximately 50-60 minutes including Q&A. The audience typically consists of members of the astrophysics group at KIAS, including Prof. Changbom Park and Dr. Stephen Appleby, and roughly 10 international postdocs working mainly on cosmology and galaxy evolution (often together with participants joining via Zoom).
> We can offer a modest honorarium, and we usually have dinner together after the meeting. We are currently considering {{date option 1}}, {{date option 2}}, or {{date option 3}} for the meeting.
>
> We would be happy to accommodate your availability. If you are interested, please let me know your preferred date.
> Thank you very much for your time and consideration. I look forward to hearing from you.
>
> Best wishes,
> {{Your Name}}

## 2. After acceptance

> Thank you very much for accepting our invitation. We're delighted to have you as a speaker at the upcoming KDESci meeting. We typically have around 10-15 participants, and the atmosphere is a bit informal; people are welcome to ask questions at any time during the talk. Given the broad range of backgrounds in the audience, I would appreciate it if you could include sufficient introductory material in your talk.
>
> It would be great if you could send me your talk title and abstract by {{deadline date, e.g. May 22 (Fri)}}, so that I can make an announcement in advance. I will send you a reminder beforehand. Also, it would be helpful if you could share your CV for the speaker introduction and paperwork.
>
> I look forward to your visit to KIAS in {{month}}.
